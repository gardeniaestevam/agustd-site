Story

Min Yoon-gi, known professionally by his stage names Suga and Agust D, is a South Korean rapper, songwriter and record producer.

  • One of the most awarded korean singers
  • Five times Grammy nominee
  • Most awarded korean record producer
  • Artist with the most songs at the top of the Billboard Hot 100 in the decade

Min Yoon-gi was born on March 9, 1993, in Daegu, South Korea. The stage name Suga is derived from the first syllables of the term shooting guard, the position he played in basketball as a student. He adopted the alias Agust D in 2016 for his mixtape, which is derived from the initials DT for his birthplace, Daegu Town, and "Suga", spelled backwards.
